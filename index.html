The 6th International Conference on Applied Category Theory will take place at the University of Maryland in College Park, Maryland, United States from 31 July to 4 August, preceded by the Adjoint School 2023. This conference follows previous events at Florida (2025), Oxford (2023), Maryland (2023), Strathclyde (2022), Cambridge (2021), MIT (2020), Oxford (2019) and Leiden (2018).
